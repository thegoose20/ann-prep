Scope and Contents
'The records are composed of: minutes, 1886-1890, and 1919-1990; miscellaneous correspondence, 1986-1990, and a mailing list master copy; particular letters from James Harvey to R. E. McIntyre, 1945, and from Hugh Falconer to W. T. Cairns, 1931; syllabus, 1886-1990, and duplicate copies; accounts books, 1923-1950 and 1963-1991; and, papers given at the Edinburgh Theological Club, 1893-1925.'

Biographical / Historical
'The Edinburgh Theological Club was founded in the 1880s and continued throughout the 20th century.'
