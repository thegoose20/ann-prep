Biographical / Historical
'Alfred Daniell was born in Llanelly. He studied at Edinburgh University and graduated with the degree of MA in 1874. Daniell also obtained the degrees of B.Sc. in 1877, D.Sc. in 1884, and LL.B. in 1885. He published textbooks on physics. Alfred Daniell died on 12 January 1937.'
