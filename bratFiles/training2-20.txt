Scope and Contents
"The item contains musical notation to some short pieces including: 'White wings'; 'Dinah Doe'; duet from 'Ruddigore''; the 'Reel of Tulloch'; 'The Happy Fatherland'; and, 'Mia bella'."

Biographical / Historical
'The bound volume of musical manuscript that forms this collection is noted with the name: Kate M. Cowan, Valleyfield, Penicuik, 1888.'

Processing Information
'Compiled by Graeme D. Eddie, Special Collections, Edinburgh University Library.'
