Scope and Contents
"This thesis of 296 pages was probably written in the mid- to late-1950s and is written in language that reflects the period. It features a bibliography of works from between 1902 and 1954, and the work is concerned with the black community and assimilation within the 'bluebrick universities' of Oxford and Cambridge."

Biographical / Historical
'The thesis - An analysis of the assimilation of African and West Indian students in the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge - written by Sheila Webster, is a study of minorities. One copy of the title was previously located at the Edinburgh University Social Sciences Research Centre. It is not known to which institution the work was submitted.'
