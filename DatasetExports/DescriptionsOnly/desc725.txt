'Correspondence from John Ronaldson to family in Fife, Scotland, both during his journey to North America and from East Braintree in Massachusetts USA, and from Skagticoke (Schaghticoke). The material is in the form of typescript copies and photostat copies of manuscript letters.'


'John Ronaldson emigrated to North America in the 1850s, travelling from Greenock, on the River Clyde, Scotland.'


'Compiled by Graeme D. Eddie, Edinburgh University Library, Special Collections Division.'
