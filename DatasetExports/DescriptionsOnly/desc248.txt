'This is a manuscript volume, written on paper, and has 231 leaves, measuring 240mm by 170mm. It is bound in contemporary vellum over pasteboards.'


"This volume of 'Philosophical tracts' was written by Felix Augier while a student at the Jesuit College of Sainte Marthe, in Poitiers, in 1649. It was formerly part of Bergendal Collection (MS.106) which had been owned by Joseph Pope (1921-2010)."


'Compiled by Graeme D. Eddie, Edinburgh University Library, Special Collections.'
