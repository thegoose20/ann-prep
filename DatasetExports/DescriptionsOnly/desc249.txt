'The item has 263 leaves, and measures 115mm by 75mm. It is in a fine calligraphic bookhand, rubrics and headings in red, black morocco over pasteboards.'


'This is a 17th century miniature prayerbook in manuscript on vellum, in Latin. It is probably from Trier, Germany. The volume has a modern bookplate of the Bibliotheca Phillippica - from the collection formed by Thomas Phillipps (Bt), MS.704, changed to 23866 - and it was also part of Bergendal Collection (MS.23) which had been owned by Joseph Pope (1921-2010).'


'Compiled by Graeme D. Eddie, Edinburgh University Library, Special Collections.'
